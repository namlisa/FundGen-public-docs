# Updating Contact Status

## When to Update a Contact's Status

You should update a contact's status in the FundGen Ambassador App when their engagement or situation changes. This ensures your campaign data is accurate and helps you manage your outreach effectively.

Examples include:

*   A contact who previously indicated "Not interested" now wishes to donate.
*   A contact who "Promised to donate" has now "Donated".
*   A contact previously marked with "Wrong contact details" is now reachable.
*   You need to re-engage with a contact from your archived list.

## Steps to Update Status

1.  Open the contact's profile in the FundGen Ambassador App.
2.  Tap **Change Status** or the current status indicator.
3.  Select the new status from the list of available options:
    *   Contacted
    *   No reply to contact
    *   Promised to donate
    *   Donated
    *   Not interested
    *   Wrong contact details
    *   Contact later
    *   Maybe donate in the future
    *   Donated by other
    *   Thanked
4.  (Optional but recommended)
