# Campaign Setup Guide

Welcome!  
To get started, please complete the initial setup process. This setup only needs to be done once and will prepare your campaign to run smoothly in the system.

You can access the Admin Interface here:  
[https://admin.givingl.ink/](https://admin.givingl.ink/)

All **platform campaign managers** already have access to the admin dashboard.  
Managers from the **organization side** will only get access if either:

- They become ambassadors in the campaign, or
- They are added as managers in **Step 2** during this setup process.

---

## Step 1 - Campaign Dates

**Define your campaign timeline**

In this step you will set the timeframe of your campaign.

Please select:

- Campaign start date
- Campaign end date

Make sure the dates are correct, as they determine when the campaign will run and how activity will be tracked in the system.

After entering the dates, click **Continue**.

---

## Step 2 - Partners & Managers

**Add managers who will have dashboard access**

In this step you can add managers who will have access to the **Manager Dashboard**.

These users will be able to manage and edit the content that appears in the app.

For each manager you can add:

- Name
- Phone number

Once added, these managers will have access to the system.

If you do not need additional managers, you can **skip this step**.

---

## Step 3 - WhatsApp Connection

**Connect your WhatsApp to the system**

This is one of the most important steps in the setup process.

The system uses WhatsApp to communicate with donors and manage campaign activity, so connecting your WhatsApp instance is required for full functionality.

Important recommendation:  
It is **highly recommended to perform this step from a computer**, as connecting WhatsApp is much easier from desktop.

What to do:

1. Open the WhatsApp connection screen
2. Follow the instructions to connect your WhatsApp instance
3. If a QR code appears, scan it using WhatsApp on your phone
4. Wait for confirmation that the connection was successful

Once WhatsApp is connected successfully, continue to the next step.

---

## Step 4 - Content & Media

**Upload campaign content and media**

In this step you can upload the content that will be used during the campaign.

Examples include:

- Images
- Videos
- Campaign materials
- Message templates

These assets will be used when communicating with donors and promoting the campaign.

If you do not want to upload content now, you can **skip this step** and return later.

---

## Step 5 - Potential Donors Database

**Add potential donor contacts**

Here you can upload or add a list of potential donors.

You may:

- Upload a contact list file
- Add contacts manually

This list will help the system organize outreach and donor communication.

If you do not have this information yet, you can **skip this step** and add contacts later.

---

## Step 6 - Tap to Pay

**Enable payment options**

In the final step you can configure the payment options for your campaign.

This allows donors to contribute quickly and easily through the system.

If payment configuration is not required at this stage, you may **skip this step** and complete it later.

---

## Setup Complete

Once the steps above are completed, your campaign will be ready to use.

You will then be able to manage the campaign, communicate with donors, and monitor activity directly from the dashboard.
